Welcome | HackEmory Group

Welcome to HackEmory, the premier undergraduate programming and computer engineering club at Emory University. If you are interested in upcoming events, feel free to join our Facebook group to stay in the loop.

Go to Facebook Group

Who We Are

  • HackEmory is a student-run organization whose aim is to provide an avenue for all university students to develop technical solutions to societal issues.
  • We aim to be as diverse as possible, both in personal backgrounds and technical skillsets. It doesn't matter if you're a math quadruple-major or if you study underwater basketweaving. If you want to code, you are welcome here.

What We Do

  • We will host our sixth annual eponymous hackathon next semester.
  • We are launching a Hacking Initiative, designed to provide mentorship and funding for undergraduate software/hardware projects.
  • We organize weekly General Body Meetings, where students lead demos, workshops, and other structured programming sessions.
  • We train and arrange teams for the national ACM competitive programming competition.

Dev Initiative

Dev Initiative is a new program being implemented this semester. The goal is to provide more structure and mentorship to semester-long projects, for those who want it.

Below is a list of current projects with established project leaders. If you have your own project idea that you would like to lead or get funded, please contact Chris Wyllie.

  • Web Dev Crash Course
    • Overview for basic HTML/CSS, eventually javascript libraries and backends
    • How to obtain and maintain domains
  • Python Web Scraper
    • How to locally save and use data from websites with Python
    • Use of Python libraries (Beautiful Soup, Selenium, etc.)
  • DIY SmartMirror / Motion Tracking Nerf Gun
    • Modding hardware to communicate with microcomputers (Raspberry Pi / Arduino)
    • Use of handtools
  • Facebook Messenger Bot
    • Building FAQ bot on Facebook Messenger app to help with our Spring Hackathon
    • Use of Python, hosted on Heroku
  • Oculus Rift VR
    • Game/app development for Oculus
    • Use of Unity and Blender, but open to suggestions
  • The Infirmary
    • Diagnosing/repairing club-owned hardware
    • 3D printer, drone, LED board
